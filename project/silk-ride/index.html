Silk Road

I decided to cycle across the Silk Road. You may ask why not just fly to Istanbul, why bother riding, why take risks, why spend so much money and time. To me, it’s got something almost similar to why I dream to become an astronaut. It’s the unknown that makes it attractive. It’s the challenge that motivates me. It’s the vast nature I want to feel. It’s the warmth of people I want to encounter. It’s the norm I want to question. It’s the borders I want to forget. And that’s a life I want to live.

The route from Beijing to Rome for the bike touring trip.
